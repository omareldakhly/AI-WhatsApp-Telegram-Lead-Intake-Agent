# AI WhatsApp & Telegram Lead Intake Agent - Case Study

## Project Overview

This project is an AI-powered lead intake automation system designed to collect leads from WhatsApp and Telegram, analyze their messages using AI, organize the lead data, and notify the business team instantly.

The goal of this project is to reduce manual work, speed up customer response time, and make sure no potential lead is missed.

---

## The Problem

Many businesses receive customer inquiries from multiple channels such as WhatsApp and Telegram. Managing these leads manually can cause several problems:

- Slow response time
- Missed messages
- Unorganized lead data
- Repeated manual data entry
- Difficulty identifying serious customers
- No clear follow-up process

This can waste time and reduce the chance of converting leads into paying customers.

---

## The Solution

I built an automation workflow that receives incoming messages from WhatsApp and Telegram, sends the message content to an AI model for analysis, extracts the important lead information, saves the data in a structured format, and notifies the team immediately.

The system helps businesses respond faster and manage leads more efficiently.

---

## Workflow

The automation follows this process:

1. A customer sends a message through WhatsApp or Telegram.
2. The automation receives the message.
3. The message is sent to AI for analysis.
4. AI extracts and classifies important information such as:
   - Customer name
   - Contact details
   - Service requested
   - Lead intent
   - Lead priority
   - Suggested reply
5. The lead data is saved into a CRM or spreadsheet.
6. The team receives an instant notification.
7. The business can follow up faster with the customer.

## AI Role in the Automation

AI is used to understand the customer message and convert unstructured text into useful business data.

For example, instead of a team member reading every message manually, the AI can detect:

- What the customer wants
- How serious the lead is
- What service they are asking about
- Whether the message needs urgent follow-up
- A suggested response for the team

This makes the lead handling process faster and more accurate.

## Example Use Case

A customer sends a message on WhatsApp asking about a service and pricing.

The automation captures the message, sends it to AI, extracts the customer request, classifies the lead as warm or hot, saves the information in Google Sheets or CRM, and sends a notification to the team with a short AI summary.

This allows the team to reply quickly with the right information.
